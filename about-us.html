We value... 

Devoted Friendship

Just like the bond between you and your dachshund: We are there for you. We care about you and your dog, being there in both joyful and challenging moments. We want to build a relationship that goes beyond basic necessities like going for a walk or serving food, focussing on support and care.

Trusted Support

Caring for your dachshund comes with its challenges, the attitude, the health problems, we know it all. But you are not in it alone. Through in-depth research and thoughtful design, we are here to support you. Whether it’s understanding their unique needs or providing expert-backed support, we’re committed to being a trusted partner in your journey.

Lively expression

We believe that Dachshund back braces shouldn't be just functional. They should also be stylish, fun, vibrant, and eye-catching. Most importantly, they should be gritty. Courageous and brave, same as the Dachshunds are. They should bring joy not only to your dog, but also to you.

Proactive Care

We value preventative care. Gritty doesn't only respond to the problem but also anticipates and prevents it. We motivate dachshunds' owners to take proactive action, because true care means being prepared.
